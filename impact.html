Impact Reflection

Advocacy Title: “Think Before You Share”

1. Which post was most effective? Why?

The engagement post (question/poll) was the most effective because it encouraged people to participate and share their opinions. Many responded honestly about their experiences with fake news. Since it allowed interaction, more people became aware of the importance of checking information before sharing. It created discussion, which made the advocacy stronger and more meaningful.

2. What difficulties did you encounter?

One difficulty I encountered was encouraging people to participate and respond to my posts. Some people viewed the content but did not comment or react. Another challenge was making sure my information was accurate and reliable. I had to double-check sources to ensure that I was not spreading misinformation while promoting media literacy.

3. Did your advocacy influence people?

Yes, my advocacy influenced people in a simple but meaningful way. Some of them said they learned the importance of verifying information before sharing. Others mentioned that they would start reading full articles instead of just the headlines. Even small changes in their behavior show that the advocacy made an impact
