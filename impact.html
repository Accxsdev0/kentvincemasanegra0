Impact Reflection

Advocacy Title: “Think Before You Share”

1. Which post was most effective? Why?

The engagement post (question/poll) was the most effective because it encouraged people to participate and share their opinions. Many responded honestly about their experiences with fake news. Since it allowed interaction, more people became aware of the importance of checking information before sharing.

2. What difficulties did you encounter?

One difficulty I encountered was encouraging people to participate and respond to my posts. Some viewed the content but did not comment or react. Another challenge was ensuring that my information was accurate and reliable, so I had to double‑check sources to avoid spreading misinformation.

3. Did your advocacy influence people?

Yes, my advocacy influenced people in a simple but meaningful way. Some said they learned the importance of verifying information before sharing, while others mentioned they would start reading full articles instead of only headlines. Even small behavioral changes show that the advocacy made an impact.
